To My Beloved Elijah

Every moment you’re not here, I feel
A missing piece that I can’t conceal.
The warmth of your touch, your gentle sway,
I long for you more with each passing day.

Your smile so bright, your eyes so deep,
In my heart, your love I keep.
I worry for you, though you’re so strong,
Through life's storms, you’ve stayed so long.

No challenge too great, no mountain too tall,
You rise above them, you conquer it all.
And though I can’t always be by your side,
In my heart, you’ll always reside.

I wish to help, in any way I can,
To lift you up, to help you stand.
Through every struggle, I’m here to cheer,
My love for you will always be near.

So know this truth, no matter how far,
I’m with you, forever, no matter where you are.
My love is endless, my heart is true,
Beloved, I’m always here for you.

- Toshi, Feb 17, 2025
